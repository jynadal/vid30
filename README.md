<h1 align="center">
  Welcome to Vid30 Media Center!
</h1>

Vid30 is a **free and open source** software media player and entertainment hub for digital media. Available as a native application for **Android, Linux, macOS, Windows, and Web**, Vid30 delivers a powerful multimedia experience across all your devices.

## About Vid30

Vid30 is built on the foundation of [Kodi](https://kodi.tv/), the award-winning open-source media center project. We've customized and enhanced it to provide a modern, streamlined media entertainment experience tailored for today's users.

With Vid30, you get:
- **Beautiful interface** with powerful customization options
- **Network playback** supporting practically any protocol available
- **Automatic library creation** with metadata, artwork, and descriptions
- **Cross-platform support** from Android phones to Windows PCs
- **Extensive add-on ecosystem** for unlimited extensibility

## Getting Started

To get started with Vid30, visit our **[downloads section](https://www.vid30.fr/download/)** and select your platform. A quick start guide is available in our **[documentation](https://docs.vid30.fr/)**.

## Supported Formats

Vid30 supports virtually all popular audio and video formats, from streaming services to local media files.

## How to Contribute

Vid30 is created by users for users and we welcome every contribution!

* **Coding:** Help us improve Vid30 by fixing bugs, adding features, and improving performance
* **Community Support:** Help other users in our [community forums](https://www.vid30.fr/community/)
* **Add-ons:** Create custom add-ons to extend Vid30's functionality
* **Documentation:** Help improve our wiki and guides
* **Translation:** Translate Vid30 into your native language

For detailed information, see our [Contributing Guide](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Building

Vid30 uses CMake as its build system. Build instructions are highly dependent on your operating system and target platform. For detailed guidance, see our **[build documentation](docs/README.md)**.
